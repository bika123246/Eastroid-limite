Services we provide

We specialise in complete installations of electrical services. We also carry out routine maintenance services to existing electrical installations. Our in house services include data and electrical cabling installation, thermal image Testing and documentation of implementation. We also install general power systems, earthing and bonding as well as normal and emergency lighting.

UPS Installation (UPS - Uninterruptable Power Supply)

Eastroid has qualified engineers to carry out UPS installations and services that include UPS system repair and maintenance services. Our engineers are experienced in installing single and three phase systems. Additionally, installations of online three phase power protection, large capacity and emergency lighting systems can be repaired. Our engineers are also capable of troubleshooting, battery replacement and preventive maintenance.

Electrical Equipment Installation

Eastroid can provide electrical installations for all types of electrical equipment including power sockets, lighting, power distribution boards, main switch boards and flood lighting.
